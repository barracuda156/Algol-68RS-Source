Ex 2.6
Your answer should be something like this:- 
   PROGRAM ex2 6 CONTEXT VOID
   USE standard
   BEGIN
      print(-7 MOD 3);
      print( 7 MOD -3);
      print(-7 MOD -3)
   END
   FINISH
This will display 
         +2          +1          +2
on your screen.
